Puzzles

You know that feeling when life is easy and your problems yield at the slightest touch? Yeah, the things on this page aren't like that. If you like a challenge, you'll probably like some of these puzzles.

I've been making puzzle hunts since high school. As a senior at Virginia Tech, I started the VT Hunt, which grew into a (still ongoing!) university tradition, with later Hunts drawing >1000 participants. A few of my favorite puzzles I've made are below. They're roughly ordered from easiest to hardest, so you can pick where to start.
