Blog

I started to put my writing on this website early in my PhD. I've found it very useful, and it serves a few purposes: it's a repository of research ideas and intuitions not quite baked enough for a paper; it's a way of sharing thoughts and updates about other parts of my life; it's a place to put fun writing; it's a reason to write at all, and writing teaches one how to think clearly.
