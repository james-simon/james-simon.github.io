title: Take the Aphorism Train
origin: NeurIPS 2025
answer: eden

Every train line traces out a common aphorism, with every stop denoting a different word. Glyphs give hints for some of the words. The five numbered stops read out HOME FOR THE FIRST MAN, which is EDEN.
